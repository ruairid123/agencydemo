What We Serve

From the first espresso pull to the last slice of quiche — made with care, every single day.

Specialty Coffee

We take our coffee seriously. From single-origin espresso to silky flat whites and rich Americanos, every cup is pulled with precision and poured with care. Because your morning deserves better than ordinary.

Fresh-Baked Pastries

Our ovens are on before the sun rises. Warm cinnamon rolls, buttery croissants, and seasonal bakes — made fresh in-house each morning. Once they're gone, they're gone.

Savoury Quiches

Hearty, golden, and packed with flavour — our quiches are made from scratch daily using locally sourced ingredients. A proper sit-down bite that's worth every minute of your lunch break.

Our Story

A little café with a lot of heart, right here on Killarney Road.

  • The Idea

    A Coffee Shop Kenmare Deserved

    Bean & Batch started with a simple belief: every town as special as Kenmare deserves a café that takes real care over its food and coffee. We set our sights on Killarney Road and got to work turning that belief into something you could taste.

  • Opening Day

    Throwing Open the Doors

    We opened on a crisp Kerry morning and welcomed Kenmare in. The cinnamon rolls sold out before 9am. We've been baking extra ever since — though we make no promises they'll last much longer.

  • The Menu Takes Shape

    More Than Just Coffee

    From a strong specialty coffee base we built a menu we're genuinely proud of — seasonal pastries, golden quiches, and daily bakes that keep regulars coming back. Everything made in-house, every morning, from scratch.

  • Part of Kenmare

    A Familiar Face on Killarney Road

    Today, Bean & Batch is woven into the fabric of Kenmare mornings. It's where locals start their day, visitors slow down, and neighbours catch up over a proper cup. We wouldn't have it any other way.
